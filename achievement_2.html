Basic Robotics Workshop

  • Organizer: KURC
  • Workshop date: May 1,2- 2024

I recently had the opportunity to participate in a basic robotics workshop organized by KURC, where we explored the fundamentals and practical applications of robotics. The workshop featured hands-on activities that allowed us to understand how robotics can be applied in real-life scenarios.

A key takeaway was learning how to enhance robot communication by enabling message transfer through a Bluetooth module. Additionally, we gained valuable experience in automation by learning how to control servo motors, an essential component in robotic movement.

This experience significantly broadened my understanding of basic robotics, providing a solid foundation and opening up exciting possibilities for future learning and development in the field.
