Educational IoT Workshop

  • Organzer: KURC X KUCC
  • Workshop date: June 30 - July 2, 2024
  • Teammates During Workshop: Aryan Basel, Arekh Shrestha, Utsab Basnet, Utsab Rimal

I recently had the opportunity to participate in an educational IoT workshop organized by KURC, where we delved into the practical applications of NodeMCU. The workshop included hands-on activities that allowed us to explore how IoT can be used in real-life scenarios. A key takeaway was our ability to improve wireless communication by enabling message transfer through a Bluetooth module.

Additionally, the workshop provided valuable insights into automation by guiding us in controlling servo motors. This experience significantly expanded my knowledge in IoT, offering a strong foundation and unlocking exciting possibilities for future exploration and development in the field.
