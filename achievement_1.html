Science Expo V1

  • Organizer: Oxford Collage Of Engineering & Management
  • Event date: 23 Februry, 2023
  • Teammates: Hardik Thapaliya, Chiranjibi Gautam, Juber Alam

I recently had the incredible opportunity to participate in Science Expo V1, organized by OCEM. Together with my team, we developed Kancha Bot, a food-serving robot designed to enhance the dining experience. The robot features 8 servos for head and arm movements, a 3x16 display for the food menu, and QR code scanning functionality for ordering, providing an interactive and innovative food service solution.

Although we secure Third position at the event, the experience was highly enriching, emphasizing the importance of teamwork and creativity. This project not only expanded my knowledge in robotics but also inspired me to continue exploring innovative solutions in technology.
